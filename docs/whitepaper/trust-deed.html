TGDP Gold Custody Trust

Article I - Establishment of Trust

This Trust Deed ("Deed") is made and executed by TROT Gold Pvt. Ltd. ("Settlor" and "Trustee") for the purpose of establishing a trust ("TGDP Gold Custody Trust") to hold, manage, and safeguard physical gold assets underlying the Tokenized Gold Depository Points (TGDP) issued within the TGDP Ecosystem.

Trust Name: TGDP Gold Custody Trust

Trust Type: Private Irrevocable Trust

Governing Law: Laws of India

Article II - Purpose of Trust

The Trust is established for the following purposes:

  • To hold physical gold corresponding to all TGDP tokens in circulation
  • To ensure 100% backing of TGDP tokens with physical gold
  • To facilitate the redemption of TGDP tokens for physical gold or equivalent value
  • To maintain transparent and auditable records of gold holdings
  • To protect the interests of TGDP token holders

Article III - Trust Assets

3.1 Primary Assets: Physical gold bullion of 999 fineness (24 karat) stored in approved vaults.

3.2 Storage Facilities: Gold shall be stored in:

  • LBMA-approved vaults in India
  • Bank-operated secure vaults with insurance coverage
  • Third-party custodians meeting international standards

3.3 Ratio Maintenance: The Trust shall maintain a minimum ratio of 1 gram of pure gold for every 10 TGDP tokens in circulation.

Article IV - Trustee Duties

The Trustee shall:

  • Maintain accurate records of all gold holdings and TGDP tokens
  • Conduct monthly audits of gold reserves
  • Publish quarterly attestation reports
  • Ensure insurance coverage for stored gold
  • Process redemption requests within 7 business days
  • Report any discrepancies to regulatory authorities

Article V - Beneficiaries

5.1 Primary Beneficiaries: All holders of TGDP tokens ("Token Holders")

5.2 Rights of Beneficiaries:

  • Right to redeem TGDP for physical gold or INR equivalent
  • Right to access audit reports
  • Right to participate in governance decisions
  • Right to file complaints through the Ombudsman

Article VI - Audit & Transparency

6.1 Internal Audit: Monthly verification of gold holdings

6.2 External Audit: Annual audit by Big 4 accounting firm

6.3 Blockchain Recording: All minting and burning events recorded on Polygon blockchain

6.4 Public Dashboard: Real-time display of total TGDP supply and gold reserves

Article VII - Amendment

This Trust Deed may only be amended with:

  • Approval of 75% of TGDP token holders by value
  • 30-day public notice period
  • Regulatory approval where required

Article VIII - Termination

The Trust may only be terminated upon:

  • Complete redemption of all TGDP tokens
  • Merger with another approved trust
  • Regulatory order

Upon termination, all gold assets shall be distributed to Token Holders proportionally.
