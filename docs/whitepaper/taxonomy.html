Asset Taxonomy

💰 TGDP (Tokenized Gold Depository Point)

Unit Value: 1 TGDP = 0.1 gram pure gold
Minting Rate: 10 TGDPs per gram
Trading Fee: 0%
Benchmark: LBMA Gold Rate

TGDPs are the primary unit of value in the ecosystem, backed by earmarked physical gold held by households.

🔄 FTR (Future Trade Rights)

Swap Commission: 4%
Categories: Hospitality, Healthcare, Education, Retail, Travel
Redemption: At partner establishments
Validity: 12 months from swap

FTRs represent pre-paid credits redeemable at partner establishments across various service categories.

🎫 GIC (Gold Income Coupon)

Revenue Share: 25% to Licensee
Income Streams: 3 (Registration, Minting, Trading)
Redemption: Cash at LBMA rates
License Cost: $300 USD per household

GICs represent Licensee earnings from their registered household activities.
