DESIGNER IPR AGREEMENT

T-JDB Intellectual Property Rights Agreement

1. IPR REGISTRATION

All designs uploaded to T-JDB will be registered for IPR protection.

Designer retains full ownership of their designs.

Company acts as licensing agent for the designs.

2. REVENUE SHARING

Designer Share: 85% of design license fee

Platform Commission: 15% of design license fee

3. DESIGN PROTECTION

Blockchain-based timestamp for design registration

WIPO integration for international protection

Automated infringement monitoring

4. PROHIBITED CONTENT

  • Copied or plagiarized designs
  • Culturally offensive imagery
  • Trademarked symbols without permission
