DESIGNER IPR AGREEMENT

T-JDB Intellectual Property Rights Agreement

Agreement No: IPR-[AUTO] | Date: [DATE]

1. COPYRIGHT RETENTION

1.1 The Designer retains full and exclusive copyright ownership of all designs uploaded to T-JDB. This Agreement does not constitute an assignment of copyright.

1.2 The Company is granted a non-exclusive, worldwide license to display, market, and facilitate the licensing of the Designer's work on the T-JDB platform only.

1.3 The Designer may withdraw their designs at any time, subject to fulfillment of any outstanding purchase orders already in progress.

1.4 The Designer warrants that all uploaded designs are original works and do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

2. BLOCKCHAIN REGISTRATION

2.1 Upon upload, each design is assigned a unique keccak256 hash computed from its content and registered on the Polygon blockchain via the IPRRegistry smart contract.

2.2 The on-chain record provides an immutable, timestamped proof of existence for the design, establishing priority of creation.

2.3 Design metadata is additionally pinned to IPFS via Pinata, creating a permanent, content-addressed record. The IPFS CID is recorded in the blockchain entry.

2.4 The Designer may verify their on-chain registration at any time via Polygonscan using the transaction hash provided at registration.

3. REVENUE SHARING

Designer Share: 85% of each design license fee

Platform Commission: 15% of each design license fee

Payment Currency: TGDP tokens, credited to Designer's balance within 24 hours of each sale

INR Option: Buyers may also purchase via Razorpay (INR); Designer receives equivalent TGDP

4. DESIGN PROTECTION

  • Blockchain-based timestamp on Polygon (IPRRegistry contract)
  • IPFS content-addressed storage (Pinata) for tamper-proof metadata
  • Hash comparison for automated duplicate/infringement detection
  • WIPO integration for international protection (future roadmap)

5. PROHIBITED CONTENT

  • Copied or plagiarized designs
  • Culturally offensive imagery
  • Trademarked symbols without permission
  • Designs previously registered by another party on-chain

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

6.1 IPR disputes shall be submitted through the T-JDB portal and routed to the Ombudsman.

6.2 On-chain registration timestamp serves as primary evidence of design ownership priority.

6.3 Resolution timeline follows the standard SLA: acknowledgment within 48 hours, resolution within 14 days.
