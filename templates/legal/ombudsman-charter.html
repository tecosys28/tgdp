OMBUDSMAN CHARTER

TGDP Ecosystem Dispute Resolution Framework

1. ROLE OF OMBUDSMAN

The Ombudsman serves as an independent and impartial authority for resolving disputes within the TGDP Ecosystem.

  • Receive and investigate complaints
  • Mediate between parties
  • Issue binding resolutions
  • Recommend systemic improvements

2. INDEPENDENCE

The Ombudsman operates independently of all other roles in the ecosystem. An Ombudsman cannot hold any other role (Household, Licensee, Jeweler, Designer, etc.).

3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS

StageTimelineDescription
FilingDay 0Complaint submitted through portal; case ID issued immediately
Acknowledgment48 hoursReceipt confirmed, Ombudsman assigned
Investigation7 daysFacts gathered, evidence reviewed
Mediation10 daysParties brought together for resolution
Resolution14 daysFinal decision issued
Appeal Window7 daysPeriod to contest resolution

4. COMPLAINT CATEGORIES

  • T-Gold: Minting issues, trading disputes, FTR problems, withdrawal delays
  • GIC: Payment disputes, household conflicts, license issues, commission disputes
  • T-JR: Valuation disputes, payment issues, pickup problems, quality complaints
  • T-JDB: IPR violations, order disputes, payment issues, designer complaints

5. RESOLUTION POWERS

  • Order refunds up to the disputed amount
  • Reverse erroneous transactions
  • Suspend violating accounts
  • Recommend license revocation to Admin
  • Award compensation for proven damages
