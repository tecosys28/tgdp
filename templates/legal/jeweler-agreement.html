JEWELER PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

T-JR Network Participation Agreement

1. ELIGIBILITY

  • Valid BIS License for gold hallmarking
  • NABL Accreditation for gold assaying
  • Minimum 3 years in gold jewelry business
  • Clean regulatory record

2. JEWELER SERVICES

  • Gold purity assessment for T-JR returns
  • Issue purity certificates
  • Participate in home pickup network
  • Process TGDP/cash payments

3. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

Assessment Fee: 0.5% of assessed gold value

Home Pickup Fee: ₹200 per visit (paid by customer)

4. QUALITY STANDARDS

All assessments must be accurate within ±0.5% purity tolerance.

Disputes regarding assessment will be resolved by Company-appointed arbiter.
